Positives & Negatives of Utopia.

Positives 

In a utopian society there would be no need for money, which would mean no cost for living or any expensis. Everyone would be equal which could mean there would be more free will. In a perfect society there would be no crime, arguments, disagreements and people would live well in harmony. 

Negatives

If anyone were every to try to create a utopian society people would have to worry about the incentive to work which could lead to the lack of modern convniences. People would disobey the law which would result in no free will and someone would have to take control of the society. There would be many disagreements between everyone, whether what is right or wrong or who should be in power.
