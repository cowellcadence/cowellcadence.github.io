What is Utopia?

Bliss, perfection, wonderland, heaven, Utopia is described in so many ways. It’s an imaginary thought in which everything is in the state of perfection. Some people say that it’s complete freedom and others believe it’s the future. Either way, Utopia can be interpreted differently by everyone.
