My Vision of Utopia

If I were to invision my utopia, currency would not be involved. In my opinion currency leads to greed, even in my life. I want peace with everyone and no one to be greater than another. I would like a community who gives to another with open arms not expecting anything in return. A community who shares together seems to me a happy community. My inspiration for this thought is a camp that I attend every summer. As a staff member at this camp we give all children an oppertunity to experience a summer of a lifetime. At camp counsellors share memories, meals, and time with the campers. When I attend camp, that is the closest to utopia I've every been. It's so peaceful and away from my busy life. I love to experience the world with no technology. This helps me to notice more of my surroundings and I see more sunsets and rises, more nature, and a whole lot of wildlife. If I could share my experience with the entire world, I would. Unfortinuatly, some people aren't interested in living in the woods for a summer and I can tell my vision of utopia is very different from others.
